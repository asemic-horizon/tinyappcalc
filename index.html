Poor Richard's Interactive Jackknife Demonstration

Short explanation

When you take a bunch of measurements, you have a sample with deterministic properties. For example, the average is what the average is. But in the statistical setting your sample is an imperfect representation of the population; effectively, the population average is, to you, a random vriable.

The jackknife is a nonparametric plug-in estimator for the population average. The stuff below was written to learn how to communicate HTML pages with REST back-ends, but can give you useful results at any rate.
